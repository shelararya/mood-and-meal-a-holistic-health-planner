About Us

Welcome to Mood and Meal

Mood and Meal is your personal holistic health planner that helps you live in sync with your body, mind, and emotions. We believe that physical health and mental wellness are not separate journeys — they go hand-in-hand.

🌱 Our Philosophy

True wellness is more than just eating the right food. It's about understanding your emotions, responding with compassion, and nourishing yourself intentionally. We’ve created a space where nutrition, mood tracking, meditation, and mindfulness come together to create long-lasting well-being.

🍎 What We Offer

  • ✨ Mood-based meal planning
  • 🧘‍♀️ Personalized wellness routines
  • 📝 Daily emotional journaling
  • 🎧 Guided meditations and breathing exercises
  • 🥗 Healthy recipes for every mood

👩‍💼 Our Story

Mood and Meal was born out of a simple yet powerful idea: to help people feel better through small, meaningful choices. Our founder, a certified holistic health coach, struggled with emotional eating and stress-induced fatigue for years. After discovering the power of mindful eating and emotional self-awareness, she created Mood and Meal to help others on the same journey.

💡 Our Approach

Our approach is integrative. Every feature of Mood and Meal is backed by psychology, nutrition science, and ancient wellness practices like yoga and Ayurveda. We focus on helping you understand your emotional patterns and empower you to make informed decisions for your health.

🌟 Core Values

  • 🌍 Sustainability and Simplicity
  • ⚖️ Balance Over Perfection
  • 📖 Lifelong Learning
